Ummah Premier Futsal League
Player Waiver & Liability Release
Season 1 — August 22 – October 24, 2026  |  Hoops Central, Strongsville
1. Agreement to Participate

I, the undersigned, hereby voluntarily agree to participate in activities organized by the Ummah Premier Futsal League (UPFL), including but not limited to futsal matches, training sessions, and related events during the 2026 season.

I acknowledge that I have read this waiver in its entirety, understand its terms, and agree to be bound by them as a condition of my participation.

2. Assumption of Risk

I understand and acknowledge that participation in futsal and related physical activities involves inherent risks, including but not limited to:

  • Physical contact, collisions, and falls resulting in injury
  • Strains, sprains, fractures, or other musculoskeletal injuries
  • Cardiovascular exertion and related health risks
  • Injuries caused by equipment, facilities, or other participants
  • Any other risks associated with competitive indoor sports

I voluntarily assume all such risks, known and unknown, and accept full responsibility for any injury, loss, or damage that may occur during my participation.

3. Release of Liability

In consideration of being permitted to participate, I hereby release, waive, discharge, and covenant not to sue the Ummah Premier Futsal League, its organizers, volunteers, sponsors, venue owners (including Hoops Central Strongsville), and any affiliated individuals (collectively, "Released Parties") from any and all claims, demands, losses, damages, costs, or liabilities arising out of or related to my participation, whether caused by negligence of the Released Parties or otherwise.

4. Medical Authorization

In the event of a medical emergency arising during UPFL activities, I authorize the Released Parties to seek and consent to emergency medical treatment on my behalf if I am unable to do so myself. I agree to be responsible for all costs associated with such treatment.

I confirm that I am in adequate physical health to participate in competitive futsal and am not aware of any medical condition that would make such participation unsafe.

5. Code of Conduct

I agree to abide by all UPFL rules and regulations and understand that:

  • Unsportsmanlike conduct, verbal abuse, or physical aggression may result in immediate removal
  • Red cards may result in suspension from one or more matches at the discretion of UPFL administration
  • Repeated violations may result in permanent removal from the league without refund
  • All decisions by referees and UPFL officials are final during matches
6. Media Release

I grant the UPFL permission to photograph, record, and publish images or video of me taken during UPFL events for promotional, social media, and documentation purposes, without compensation or further consent required.
